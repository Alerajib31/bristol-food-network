About Bristol Food Network

Connecting local producers with the Bristol community since 2024

Our Mission

Bristol Food Network is a digital marketplace that bridges the gap between local food producers and consumers in the Bristol region. We believe in making fresh, sustainably grown food accessible to everyone while supporting the livelihoods of local farmers and artisan producers.

By cutting out the middleman, we ensure that producers get fair prices and consumers get the freshest produce possible — often harvested within 24 hours of delivery.

Sustainability First

Reducing food miles and supporting sustainable farming practices across the Bristol region.

Our Values

Community

Building stronger connections between producers and consumers in the Bristol area.

Transparency

Know exactly where your food comes from and how it was produced.

Sustainability

Reducing food waste and carbon footprint through local, seasonal produce.
